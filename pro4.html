This project applies machine learning to predict animal outcomes in shelters. By analyzing details such as species, breed, age, color, and intake type, the model predicts whether an animal will be Adopted, Transferred, or Returned to Owner. The dataset includes 29k+ real records, making it highly realistic. This helps shelters improve adoption strategies, allocate resources better, and support overall animal welfare.
